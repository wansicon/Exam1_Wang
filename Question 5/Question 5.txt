Question 5

For eukaryotes: 10^8-10^11 base pairs per haploid genome 

Yeast or fungi:10^7-10^8
Plants: 10^8 (Arabidopsis) -10^11 (Lily)
Insects(Drosophila):10^8-10^10
Amphibians: 10^9 (Toad)-10^11(Salamander)
Birds and chicker: around 10^9
Human and mammals: 10^9-10^10

For prokaryotes: 10^6-10^7 base pairs per haploid genome (E.coli, Mycoplasma et.al)
